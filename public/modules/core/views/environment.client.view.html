Environment

REDUCE-REUSE-RECYCLE

Avanti Cycling tries to abide by the above mantra. This means that:

  1. We try and reduce paperwork where possible. As business as possible will be conducted electronically
  2. We will reuse paper and envelopes where possible. This might look a little unprofessional but it fits into our environmental ethos
  3. Bikes will be re used where possible. This increases out maintenance bill but fits entirely within our REUSE policy

All trips, where possible will be made by train and bike.

Benefits:

  1. Environmental. This is an entirely environmental policy
  2. Cost. It may benefit Avanti Cycling in terms of cost
  3. Example. We hope to provide a good environmental example to others.
