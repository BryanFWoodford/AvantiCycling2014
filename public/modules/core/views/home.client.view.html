Bikeability

Bikeability is the modern way of teaching your children to ride in traffic. We use modern interactive and realistic teaching methods with highly trained instructors.

If your children are to use their bikes safely to go to their friends or ride to school then they need experience with real traffic.

Our courses gently introduce the trainees to traffic in a progressive manner. By the end of the course they will be riding competently in light traffic communicating to drivers and making their own decisions.
