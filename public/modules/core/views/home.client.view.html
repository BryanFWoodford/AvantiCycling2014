Who are we?

Avanti Cycling was founded by Greg Woodford. Greg has been working for the CTC for over ten years managing their cycle training department. During which time he:

  • Set up and created the on-road cycle training department for the CTC;
  • Helped create the National Standards for Cycletraining;
  • Helped to develop them into Bikeability;
  • Managed the first tranche of instructors;
  • Helped develop the four day NSI course;
  • Managed the first Instructor Training Assessment process;
  • Developed CTC into an Instructor training Organisation writing their courses and training their Instructor Trainers.
